(Nodes)crate::node should implement this trait in order to drive their state progression.

IMPORTANT: this trait is only concerned about progressing the state along a timeline, nodes are expected to deal with non-scheduled stops themselves, by calling stop_scheduling and managing their state machine as they wish on teardown, see Destination for an advanced case.
